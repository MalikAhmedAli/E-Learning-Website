Our Expert Teachers

Dr. Muhammad Numan

Dr. Mohammad Nauman is currently an Assistant Professor in Computer Sciences. He has a Postdoctorate from Max Planck Institute for Software Systems in application of Machine Learning in Security and Privacy. His research interests include remote attestation of distributed systems and security on smartphone platforms. He also serves under the Expert Group for JSR321: Trusted Computing API for the Java Platform. He has published 30+ articles in conferences of international repute, 12+ articles in impact factor journals and has presented his findings on several occasions to an international audience in USA, Ireland, UK, Germany, Spain, France and Malaysia. He is also an author of two books, one on search in social networks and another on application of remote attestation in real world scenarios. He maintains his personal blog at http://recluze.wordpress.com
